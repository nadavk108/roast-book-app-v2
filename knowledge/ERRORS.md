# Error Log

Log errors here as they occur. Follow the classification rules below.

## Classification Rules

- **Deterministic** (bad schema, wrong type, missing field, logic bug): conclude immediately, graduate fix to domain.md or procedural.md
- **Infrastructure** (timeout, rate limit, network, Vercel cold start): log occurrence + date, do NOT conclude until a pattern (3+ instances) emerges
- **Graduated**: once concluded, move the lesson to domain.md or procedural.md and remove from here
